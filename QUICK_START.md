## ğŸ’° LETS Marketplace

### What is LETS?
LETS (Local Exchange Trading System) lets family members exchange favors using community credits.

### How to Use
1. **Request a Favor** - Click "Request Favor" and describe what you need
2. **Set Credits** - Decide how many credits the task is worth
3. **Assign (Optional)** - Assign to a specific family member or leave open
4. **Accept Tasks** - Browse open favors and accept tasks you can help with
5. **Complete & Earn** - Mark tasks complete to transfer credits

### Your Balance
- **Positive Balance** = You've helped more than you've asked
- **Negative Balance (In Debt)** = You've asked for more help than you've given
- This is normal in LETS! It encourages community support.

### Features
- **5 Tabs**: All, Open, My Requests, My Tasks, Completed
- **Balance Dashboard**: See your balance, total earned, and total spent
- **Beautiful Cards**: See all favor details at a glance
- **Quick Actions**: Accept, Complete, Decline, Cancel with one click
